Welcome to CrowdNotes

Ditch the pencil and paper for this new notetaker!

Organize and share notes with ease.CrowdNotes is a crowdsourcing notetaking app custom for you and your classes. We provide a platform that allows you and classmates to simultaneously take and share notes, so you never miss one definition. CrowdNotes then uses your notes to provide you with an automatically generated study guide based on importance and relevance of key terms.
